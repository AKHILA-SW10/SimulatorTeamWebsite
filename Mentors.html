Olivier Germain

Olivier Germain is a Digitalization Program Director in Halliburton Digital Solutions. He initially worked in the upstream oil and gas industry in eight countries across three continents, managing rigs and asset teams in all technical, financial, legal, HR, training, marketing, and sales aspects. He continued to work in the E&P industry after that, but switched to managing software development, sales, and deployment.
